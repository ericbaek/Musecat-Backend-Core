# Musecat Data Use Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-14

This policy applies to the Musecat database and API responses made available through [musecat.app](https://musecat.app). It does not change the license for the Musecat Backend Core source code, which is governed separately by [`LICENSE`](LICENSE).

## 1. Covered data

"Musecat Data" means the collection, organisation, verification, and presentation of information in Musecat, including arcade records, game lineups, machine counts and conditions, pricing, opening hours, amenities, location notes, change history, fault reports, user contributions, and associated metadata and API responses.

Musecat Data may include material supplied by users or obtained from third parties. Third-party photographs, trademarks, names, and other material remain subject to their respective owners' rights.

## 2. Permitted use

You may browse Musecat Data through the service. You may also quote or link to an insubstantial portion of Musecat Data for personal, editorial, educational, or non-commercial research purposes, provided that you:

- clearly attribute Musecat and link to the relevant Musecat page where practical;
- do not misrepresent the source, date, or accuracy of the information; and
- comply with applicable law and the rights of third parties.

## 3. Uses requiring prior written permission

Except where mandatory law permits otherwise, you must obtain prior written permission from the Musecat maintainers before you:

- reproduce, export, distribute, sell, sublicense, or publicly provide all or a substantial part of Musecat Data;
- systematically collect, scrape, crawl, query, cache, or aggregate Musecat Data to create, update, or operate another database, directory, map, search service, or competing service;
- use Musecat Data for commercial products, services, advertising, lead generation, paid API access, or other commercial advantage;
- use automated access beyond documented API limits, bypass rate limits or access controls, or share credentials or API keys; or
- include Musecat Data in a dataset for training, evaluating, or operating a machine-learning or generative-AI system.

Permission requests should be made through the repository's contact channel and must state the intended data, purpose, distribution method, duration, and whether the use is commercial.

When you submit information, edits, fault reports, photos, or other content to Musecat, you confirm that you have the necessary rights to submit it. You grant Musecat a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free licence to host, reproduce, adapt, verify, moderate, display, distribute, and use that contribution to operate, maintain, improve, and promote Musecat.

Do not submit personal data, confidential information, or third-party material unless you have a lawful basis and the necessary permissions.

## 5. Database and source rights

Musecat asserts applicable rights in the selection, arrangement, verification, renewal, and substantial investment represented by Musecat Data. This policy does not claim ownership of underlying facts or limit rights that cannot legally be restricted.

## 6. Availability and accuracy

Arcade and game information can change without notice. Musecat Data is provided as-is and may be incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate. Do not rely on it as the sole basis for travel, purchasing, safety, or business decisions.

## 7. Changes and enforcement

Musecat may update this policy, apply reasonable technical controls, suspend abusive access, or revoke permissions for a breach. Continued use after a published update constitutes acceptance of the updated policy to the extent permitted by law.

For commercial licensing, research collaboration, or other permitted reuse arrangements, contact the Musecat maintainers before accessing or using the relevant data.
